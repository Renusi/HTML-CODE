Gudauri

The resort town Gudauri is located in Kazbegi region on the southern slopes of the Caucasus Mountains and is another great ski resort of Georgia. Hundreds of people come here in winter to enjoy outdoor activities, the picturesque scenery and just relax. It will take about two hours to reach Gudauri from the capital of Georgia, Tbilisi. Due to its favorable location, far from the metropolis and bustling towns, this resort is a great place for a family holiday. The choice of ski tracks is not very big, however, both the skiers with little experience, and true masters can find a route to their own preferences.
